WHAT IS SWOT ANALYSIS?

SWOT analysis is a strategic planning technique used to help an organization identify strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats related to business competition or project planning. Strengths and weakness are frequently internally-related, while opportunities and threats commonly focus on the external environment.
